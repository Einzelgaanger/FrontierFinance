\section{Project Overview}

\subsection{Platform Purpose}

The ESCP Network Platform serves as the central digital infrastructure for managing relationships with early-stage capital providers operating across frontier markets. The platform enables:

\bulletlist{
    \bulletitem{Network Management: Centralized directory of fund managers with detailed profiles}
    \bulletitem{Data Collection: Multi-year survey system capturing fund operations and performance}
    \bulletitem{Member Onboarding: Structured application workflow with admin review capabilities}
    \bulletitem{Knowledge Sharing: Integrated blog and content management system}
    \bulletitem{Analytics: AI-powered insights from aggregated network data}
    \bulletitem{Collaboration: Secure communication and resource sharing among network members}
}
