# SLIDE 2: The Challenge We're Solving

## The Missing Middle Problem

**Small and growing businesses (SGBs) create ~80% of formal employment in frontier markets**

Yet they face an estimated **$940 billion financing gap**

### The "Missing Middle" Businesses Are:
- Too big for microfinance
- Too small for private equity
- Too risky for traditional banks

### Our Solution:
A digital platform that connects, tracks, and empowers **100+ early-stage capital providers (ESCPs)** across Africa and emerging markets, enabling them to bridge this financing gap together.

# SLIDE 3: Platform Overview

## What Is the ESCP Fund Manager Portal?

A comprehensive digital ecosystem that serves as the **central hub** for the Collaborative for Frontier Finance's network of fund managers.

- 100+ fund manager profiles

**DATA COLLECTION**
- Multi-year survey system (2021-2024)

**INTELLIGENCE & INSIGHTS**
- AI-powered assistant (PortIQ)
- Comprehensive analytics dashboards
- Trend analysis across survey years

**COMMUNITY ENGAGEMENT**
- Member blogs and content sharing

- Peer networking features

# SLIDE 6: Survey System & Data Collection

## Comprehensive Annual Surveys (2021-2024)

### Survey Coverage:

**252+ Completed Survey Responses** across four years

**8 CORE SECTIONS:**

1. **Vehicle Information**
   - Fund structure, legal domicile, currencies

2. **Team & Leadership**
   - Staff count, principals, gender composition

3. **Geographic & Market Focus**
   - Target markets, headquarters location

4. **Investment Strategy**
   - Thesis, sectors, business stages, SDG alignment

5. **Fund Operations**
   - Pipeline sourcing, value creation priorities

6. **Fund Status & Timeline**
   - Legal entity, first close, first investment dates

7. **Investment Instruments**
   - Equity, debt, mezzanine, technical assistance

8. **Sector Focus & Returns**
   - Target IRR, actual performance, exits

### Data Use Cases:
- Annual network research reports
- Investor matchmaking
- Portfolio trend analysis
- Impact measurement
