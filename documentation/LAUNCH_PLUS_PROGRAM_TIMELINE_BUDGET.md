## Implementation Roadmap (Oct 2025 - Dec 2027)

## Program Timeline

### Phase I: Platform Setup & Cohort 1 (Oct 2025 - Mar 2026)
**Duration:** 6 months

**Activities:**
- Finalize VCC legal setup and FSC registration in Mauritius
- Onboard VCC Board and Investment Committee
- Launch digital platform (CRM, TA delivery, Learning Lab)
- Cohort 1 recruitment and selection (3-5 fund managers)
- Support Cohort 1 FSC sub-fund applications
- Deploy initial warehousing and opex capital
- Establish Learning Lab framework

**Deliverables:**
- VCC Certificate of Incorporation and Sub-Fund 1 License
- Fully operational digital platform
- 3-5 fund managers onboarded to Cohort 1
- Initial warehousing capital deployed
- Learning Lab portal launched

---

### Phase II: Cohort 2 & Program Scaling (Apr 2026 - Sep 2026)
**Duration:** 6 months

**Activities:**
- Cohort 2 recruitment and selection (5-10 fund managers)
- Continue Cohort 1 TA delivery and capital deployment
- Refine assessment methodology based on learnings
- Expand expert advisor network
- Host first major stakeholder convening
- Publish initial case studies

**Deliverables:**
- 5-10 additional fund managers onboarded
- Cohort 1 first close achievements tracked
- 2-3 case studies published

**Duration:** 6 months

**Activities:**

**Deliverables:**

- Mid-term evaluation report

**Activities:**

## Risk Management & Contingencies

### Technical Risks
- **Platform development delays:** Build contingency time, use proven tech stack
- **Integration challenges:** Early testing with fund administrator APIs
- **Data security concerns:** Implement enterprise-grade security from day one

### Operational Risks
- **Manager selection quality:** Rigorous assessment criteria, pilot testing
- **Capital deployment delays:** Flexible milestone structure, pipeline pre-qualification
- **TA delivery scalability:** Build expert network early, leverage peer learning

### Market Risks
- **LP fundraising environment:** Diversify LP targeting, emphasize impact metrics
- **Manager attrition:** Strong onboarding support, ongoing engagement
- **Portfolio performance:** Conservative underwriting, ongoing capacity support

### Mitigation Budget: 10% contingency (€1.65M) included in component allocations

## Success Criteria

**Program Success:**
- 20-30 managers onboarded and progressing toward first close
- €10M capital deployed efficiently with strong repayment rates
- Demonstrated cost reduction (40-60%) and time savings (70%)
- Portfolio impact metrics showing job growth and inclusive outcomes
- Institutional LP engagement and capital commitments secured

**Platform Success:**
- High user satisfaction (4.5+/5.0) across internal and external users
- Efficient operations (assessment time, report generation, uptime)
- Comprehensive data capture enabling insights and advocacy
- Scalable architecture supporting 100+ applications annually

**Market Success:**
- SBGF asset class viability demonstrated to institutional investors
- Information asymmetries reduced through data and research
- Domestic capital mobilization initiated (pension fund engagement)
- Policy/regulatory changes influenced by advocacy efforts
